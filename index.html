About Us

The Daylight Party believes in democracy, as it allows everyone to help decide their fate. The goal of bringing opportunity and prosperity with a free-market economy is not only possible, but attainable. Freedom is important to us and we will work to protect it and to ensure that it is a reality for all people. We understand that security is important as well and feel that it is possible to remain secure, without encroaching on the freedom of the people, as we know that true security is not attainable under oppression. We will work to create more opportunity and wealth by educating people in economics and law in schools, creating jobs within the country, and incentivizing investment in businesses. We will work towards a brighter future through policy and also through diplomacy and compassion.


9Axes

Daylighters believe that traditional political tests and compasses are either biased or simply not complex enough to accurately show ideals and beliefs, and overall where you stand in worldwide politics. The more "axes", the better. Many tests will only show two axes, one being a social scale and the other being an economic scale in terms of authoritarian or libertarian and "left" or "right", respectively. They represent a very simplified view on politics, and are not compound enough. Daylighters use the 9axes test because it includes specific issues, unlike the political compass test, for example. Our 9Axes test is shown to the right, and shows the approximate ideology of the Daylight Party. Taking the test for yourself (click here) and comparing it to the one shown here would give you a good idea of how similar the party’s ideologies are to yours.
